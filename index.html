About OLab

We aim to solve complex problems with optimal solutions and strategies. Our primary research interests include numerical optimization, operations research, and various real-world applications.

Running and Upcoming Projects

  • Optimal Transport in Practice: Applying optimal transport theory to data science and real-world problems.
  • Distributional Robust Optimization: Developing robust models under uncertainty with Wasserstein distance.
  • Time-dependent Optimization: Analyzing dynamic optimization problems in various applications.
